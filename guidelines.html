Display Name
Your public pseudonym on Nebula
⛔ Do not use your real name
Choose a unique pseudonym used only on Nebula — not on other platforms or social media.
Good examples
Miss Paris · Silver Luna · Star Guide · Mystic Rose
Title positioning
If your name includes a title (e.g., Coach, Master), move that title to your Subtitle and use Psychic as your primary identifier in the name field.
🏷
Subtitle & Expertise Tags
4 words or less — Title Case, use "and" not "&"
Formatting rules
Always use "and" instead of "&". Use consistent Title Case for all tags and subtitles.
Good subtitle examples
Love and Relationship Expert · Career Guidance and Spiritual Coach · Tarot and Astrology Reader
Off-brand words to avoid
❌ Psychology ❌ Clairvoyant ❌ Magic / Spells ❌ Consultation

✓ Psychic ✓ Tarot ✓ Chakra Healing ✓ Reading

Bio (Profile Description)
2–5 paragraphs · Personal · Warm · No emojis
📐 Structure — 3 paragraphs
Organize your bio into these three sections:
I
Invitation — How do you describe your purpose?
Start by speaking directly to the user. What do they feel when they come to you? Create an emotional connection first.
Example
"Are you searching for clarity and a deeper understanding of the choices before you? I believe every answer you seek already lives within you — my purpose is simply to illuminate that path."
II
Expertise — What do you use, and why?
Explain your tools (Tarot, Astrology, etc.) and your personal philosophy. If you use complex terms, briefly explain them.
Example
"I use Tarot because it allows me to translate the universal language of energy into practical steps for your life. I specialize in love, career, and personal growth readings."
III
Promise — What can the reader expect?
Close with a warm, direct invitation. What concrete outcome will they get after a reading with you?
Example
"You won't just leave with predictions — you'll leave with actionable steps and a restored sense of inner peace."
✍️ Tone of voice
✓ Do
Use "You" and "Your" to feel personal
Be warm, grounded, and mature
Share a genuine personal philosophy
Explain spiritual terms simply
Use em dash (—) not hyphen (-)
✗ Avoid
Opening with "Hello, dear friend!"
Corporate or robotic language
Emojis within the bio text
Promises of specific outcomes
The word "magic" or "guaranteedly"
💬
Slogan (Quote)
One short personal philosophy — Sentence case
A short, personal statement that immediately expresses your voice. Use Sentence case (not Title Case).
Good example
"You are stronger than you think, and your next step is already waiting for you to see it."

Profile Photo
Real · Clear · Professional · Trustworthy
Technical requirements
High resolution · Face sharp and clearly visible · No pixelation · Works after circular crop · Soft, even lighting · Natural skin tones · Minimal retouching
Do and Don't
✓ Do
Use a real, unedited photo
Face sharp and fully visible
Neutral, clean background
Soft, natural lighting
Direct eye contact with camera
Calm, warm expression
Professional or casual clothing
✗ Don't
AI-generated or stock photos
Face covered or cropped
Sexualized or revealing photos
Wedding / party / event setups
Logos or branding on clothing
Dark, moody, dramatic lighting
Filters or heavy retouching
⚠ Strictly avoid
Bed photos Lingerie / revealing outfits OnlyFans-style aesthetics Face paint / costumes Passport-style flat photos
